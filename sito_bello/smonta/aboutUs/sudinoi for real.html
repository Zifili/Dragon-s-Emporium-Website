About us
Our story

We are a small business focused on making cosplay props and fursuits even if these two areas are our main core businesses, we also offer a 3d printing and laser cutting service. We started in 2023 even though our activities didn't start until early 2024, and we are just a group of two people. With time we hope to increase our offerings and grow our business. We are continuously learning and fixing our mistakes in order to always achieve great results and products. We are present on many social medias and we also operate an Etsy store.

FAQ

Any Questions? Look Here

In this section, you are going to find the most asked questions we get. If you still need help, don't hesitate to contact us.

How old do I need to be to make purchases?

You must show proof that you are over 18 years old for commissions. For everything else, you just need a credit card.

How long does it take to receive my products?

It depends on where you live and the complexity of your design. Additionally, near the holiday season, you can expect delays.

Where do you ship?

We ship anywhere in Europe, you can contact us if you live somewhere else to arrange shipping.

Can i pay in installments?

Unfortunately no, you can only pay full price.
